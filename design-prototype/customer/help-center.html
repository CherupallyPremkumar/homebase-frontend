Frequently Asked Questions

Go to My Orders, click on the order, and select "Track Order". You will see real-time updates including shipping carrier and estimated delivery date.

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in original packaging. Custom or made-to-order items are non-returnable.

You can cancel before the order ships. Go to My Orders, select the order, and click "Cancel Order". Once shipped, you will need to initiate a return instead.

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after the returned item is received and inspected. The amount is credited to the original payment method.

Yes, free shipping is available on orders above ₹999. For orders below that, a flat ₹49 delivery charge applies. Express delivery is available at extra cost.

You can update your address before the order is packed. Go to My Orders, select the order, and click "Edit Address". After packing, the address cannot be changed.

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, and cash on delivery. EMI options are available on orders above ₹3,000.

Go to Account Settings and select "Delete Account" at the bottom. All active orders must be completed first. Data is permanently removed within 30 days per our privacy policy.
