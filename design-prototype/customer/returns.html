Frequently Asked Questions

Go to "My Orders" and select the order containing the item you wish to return. Click the "Return" button next to the product, select a reason for return, and submit the request. You can also click "Request New Return" on this page. Once approved, a pickup will be scheduled at your convenience.

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. UPI and wallet refunds are credited within 24 hours. Credit/debit card refunds may take 5-10 business days depending on your bank. You will receive an email and SMS notification once the refund is initiated.
