Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to Products > Add Product. Fill in the product title, description, images, pricing, and inventory details. You can save as draft or publish immediately. Make sure to add at least 3 high-quality images for better conversion.

Payments are settled every 7 business days after delivery confirmation. The amount is credited to your registered bank account. You can track all settlements under Payments > Settlements in your dashboard.

Return requests appear in Orders > Returns. You have 48 hours to accept or dispute a return. If accepted, the customer ships the item back. Once received and inspected, the refund is processed automatically. Check our Returns Policy for detailed guidelines.

Commission rates vary by category: Electronics 5-8%, Fashion 10-15%, Home & Kitchen 8-12%, Books 5%. You can view your category-specific rates under Settings > Fee Structure. Premium sellers may qualify for reduced commission rates.

Focus on fast shipping (within 24 hours), accurate product descriptions, quality packaging, and prompt customer support. Maintain a return rate below 5% and respond to customer queries within 4 hours. These factors directly impact your seller score.

Yes! You can create coupons under Marketing > Coupons, run flash sales, and use the Advertising section to promote products. Promoted products appear higher in search results and on the homepage. Set daily budgets and track performance in real time.
